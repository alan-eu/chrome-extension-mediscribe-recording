Face-to-Face Consultation

Use this page to record in-person medical consultations with your patient.

How to record:

  1. Click the Mediscribe Recorder extension icon in your browser toolbar
  2. Click "Start Recording" to begin capturing audio
  3. Conduct your consultation
  4. Click "Stop Recording" when finished
  5. Your recording will be automatically encrypted and uploaded
